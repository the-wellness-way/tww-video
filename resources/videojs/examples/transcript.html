Transcription Plugin

Transcription plugin developed by Nuevodevel.com synchronizes the transcription with the video playback, highlighting the current text being spoken. It is especially important for people who may be deaf or hard of hearing, as well as those who prefer to read the content.
A very similar solution you can find only on Vimeo player. Transcription text is taken fromvideo captions and can be multi-language.

Basic setup

<link href="../skins/treso/videojs.min.css" rel="stylesheet" type="text/css" />
<script src="../video.min.js"></script>
<script src="../nuevo.min.js"></script>
<script src="../plugins/videojs.transcript.js"></script>

<script>
	var player = videojs('player_1');
	player.nuevo({ contextMenu:false, pipButton:false });
	player.transcript();
</script>
